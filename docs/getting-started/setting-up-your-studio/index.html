Setting Up Your Studio

Last updated April 2026 · 5 min read

Before you begin

You'll need owner-level access to complete this setup. If you were invited as a staff member, ask the account owner to follow these steps or see Staff Roles & Permissions to request elevated access.

First time in Obliq? Studio setup is the first item in your onboarding checklist. Complete it before adding staff or publishing your booking page.

Step 1 — Studio profile

Your studio profile forms the foundation of your public-facing presence. Navigate to Settings → Studio Profile.

  1. Enter your Studio Name exactly as you want it displayed on booking confirmations, emails, and your public booking page.

  2. Upload your Studio Logo. Recommended size is 400 × 120 px, PNG or SVG with a transparent background. The logo appears on all client-facing emails and your booking widget.

  3. Write a short Studio Bio (up to 300 characters). This appears on your public booking page beneath your studio name.

  4. Select your Studio Type — Pilates, Yoga, or Mixed. This controls terminology throughout the platform and which report templates are shown.

Tip: Use a logo with a transparent background so it renders cleanly on both light and dark email clients.

Step 2 — Location & contact details

Location details appear on booking confirmations and generate map links for clients. Navigate to Settings → Location.

  1. Enter your full Street Address, including suite or unit number if applicable.

  2. Add your City, State/Province, Postal Code, and Country. Obliq uses this to generate Google Maps links in booking confirmations.

  3. Enter a Public Phone Number. This is displayed to clients on confirmation emails and the booking page.

  4. Add a Support Email for client-facing communications. This becomes the reply-to address on all outgoing notifications.

Step 3 — Timezone & currency

These settings affect how class times display to clients and how payments are processed. Navigate to Settings → Regional.

  1. Set your Timezone. All class schedules, appointments, and automated messages will be sent relative to this timezone.

  2. Choose your Currency. This controls how prices, membership rates, and pass costs are formatted throughout the platform. Note: once you have processed a payment you cannot change the currency without contacting support.

  3. Set your preferred Date Format (MM/DD/YYYY or DD/MM/YYYY). This affects how dates appear in reports and client-facing content.

Important: Set your timezone before creating any classes or appointments. Changing the timezone after scheduling is set up will shift the display of existing bookings.

Step 4 — Public booking page

Obliq provides a hosted booking page at booking.withobliq.com/your-slug. Navigate to Settings → Booking Page.

  1. Set your Booking URL Slug — lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens only. This becomes your studio's permanent booking URL (e.g., booking.withobliq.com/cityflow-pilates). The slug cannot be changed after your first booking is made.

  2. Toggle Public Booking on or off. While off, the page is visible only to logged-in staff — useful during setup and testing.

  3. Set a Booking Window — how far in advance clients can book and the minimum notice required. Defaults are 60 days ahead and 2 hours minimum notice.

Step 5 — Notification defaults

Configure when and how clients receive emails. Navigate to Settings → Notifications.

  1. Enable Booking Confirmation emails. These fire immediately when a booking is made and include location, time, and pre-class instructions.

  2. Set up Reminder emails. By default, reminders go out 24 hours before a class. You can add a second reminder at 1 hour before if desired.

  3. Configure Cancellation notifications. Clients receive an email if you cancel a class, along with a refund or credit notification depending on your cancellation policy.

  4. Review the Email Footer — this is auto-populated from your studio name and address. Confirm it is correct before sending your first client communication.

Tip: Send a test booking through your public booking page to verify all confirmation and reminder emails look correct before going live.

What's next

With your studio profile configured, you're ready to build out your team and schedule.
