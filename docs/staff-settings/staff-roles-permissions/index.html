Staff Roles & Permissions

Last updated April 2026 · 5 min read

Overview

Obliq has four staff roles. Each role grants a different level of access to the platform. Roles are assigned when you invite a staff member and can be changed at any time from Staff → [Staff Member] → Role.

Owner

The Owner has unrestricted access to every part of the platform. There is only one Owner per studio account.

  • Full access to all settings, billing, and payment configuration
  • Can invite, edit, and remove any staff member
  • Can view all financial reports and export data
  • Can change the studio's plan or cancel the account
  • Can transfer ownership to another staff member
Important: There can only be one Owner at a time. Transferring ownership is immediate and cannot be undone without the new owner's cooperation. Only transfer ownership if you are certain.

Manager

Managers can do everything an Owner can do except access billing, change payment settings, or manage the subscription plan.

  • Can create, edit, and cancel classes and appointments
  • Can add and edit staff members (but not remove them)
  • Can view all client records and booking history
  • Can access most reports except financial exports
  • Can issue refunds and adjust client credits
  • Cannot access Settings → Payments or change the billing plan
Tip: Use the Manager role for studio directors or senior front desk leads who need broad operational access but should not see or change billing details.

Instructor

Instructors have read-only access to the schedule and can see their own classes and bookings.

  • Can view their own upcoming class schedule and booking lists
  • Can see client names and contact details for their own classes
  • Can mark clients as checked-in during a class
  • Can view their own appointment availability settings
  • Cannot create or edit classes, passes, or membership plans
  • Cannot access client financial records or payment history
  • Cannot view other instructors' schedules or client lists

Front Desk

The Front Desk role is designed for reception staff who need to process walk-in bookings and handle check-ins, but should not access sensitive financial or management data.

  • Can view the full studio schedule (all classes, all instructors)
  • Can make bookings on behalf of clients
  • Can process check-ins and mark no-shows
  • Can view client pass and membership balances
  • Can take payments for single class drop-ins and pass purchases
  • Cannot create or edit class templates or membership plans
  • Cannot issue refunds or view revenue reports
  • Cannot access staff management settings

Inviting a staff member

Navigate to Staff → Invite Staff Member.

  1. Enter the staff member's Full Name and Email Address. The invitation will be sent to this email.

  2. Select their Role from the dropdown. You can change this after they accept the invitation.

  3. For Instructors, you can optionally pre-configure their Display Name (the name shown to clients on the booking page) and Bio before they log in for the first time.

  4. Click Send Invitation. The staff member receives an email with a secure link valid for 48 hours. If they do not accept in time, you can resend from the Staff list.
